Super Mario Bros

1. Project Description:

    A classic super mario game similar to https://supermarioemulator.com/supermario.php.
    Adding in survival mode where the player controls Mario and try not to get hit by enemies.

2. Structural Plan:

    All the characters and maps will be individual objects, with draw and move functions.
    Images and sounds will be in the folder assets

3. Algorithmic Plan:

    - Acceleration, deceleration, and gravity jump
    - Platformer terrain generation for survival mode
    - Pathfinding for enemies chasing the character in survival mode
